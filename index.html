After graduating with my associates degree in 2023, I made the life-changing decision to attend a Full-Stack web development course at UC Berkeley! Graduating in early 2024, I now code as a Front-End web developer, eager to create any and all works of art!

Currently, my favorite thing to do as a front-end developer, is venturing through masterfully created websites by my fellow colleagues! There are so many times that I've been left speechless thinking to myself ~ HOW DID THEY DO THIS ~ and ~ HOW CAN I DO THAT ~ that I immediately try to reproduce whatever it is that has inspired me. As a result, I code out of obsession towards my inspiration and to the practice of coding itself! Hence why I created my portfolio page.

Much credit to Keita Yamada for being the inspiration of this little project of mine. ~ Click his name for his portfolio ~

Despite my drive and dedication towards expanding my skills, I often long to be apart of a team dedicated towards coding ideas into life! So hopefully soon, and through my portfolio page, I'll get to be apart of something amazing!
